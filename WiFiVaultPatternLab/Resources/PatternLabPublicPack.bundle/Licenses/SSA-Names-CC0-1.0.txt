U.S. BABY NAMES DATA NOTICE

Source: U.S. Social Security Administration national baby-name aggregate.
https://www.ssa.gov/oact/babynames/limits.html

The packaged extraction was prepared from us-baby-names-2 1.0.2, whose package
metadata designates the dataset CC0-1.0:
https://creativecommons.org/publicdomain/zero/1.0/

PatternLab includes only 20,000 normalized, frequency-ranked first-name tokens.
It includes no person-linked records, dates of birth, Social Security numbers,
addresses, or other identity data.
